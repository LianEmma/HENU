Counseling: Nurturing Growth and Well-being

Since its inception in 2007, HENU has been at the forefront of improving lives within disadvantaged communities, with a particular focus on older persons and orphans.

As a proud member of HelpAge International, we are recognized for our innovative approaches and commitment to community capacity building. Our Counseling Center embodies this dedication by offering comprehensive support tailored to the needs of individuals across all ages.


couseling and skillbuilding Key Areas

Key Areas of Counseling :


At HENU, our counseling services are designed to address a wide range of concerns and support individuals through various life challenges. Our experienced counselors specialize in the following key areas:


Emotional and Mental Health

We provide support for conditions such as anxiety, depression, stress, and emotional distress. Our goal is to help individuals understand and manage their emotions, develop coping strategies, and improve overall mental well-being.

Life Transitions:

Major life changes—such as retirement, moving to a new city, or dealing with loss—can be challenging. We offer guidance and support to help individuals navigate these transitions and adapt to new circumstances with resilience.

Crisis Intervention:

In times of acute distress or emergency, our crisis intervention services provide immediate support and guidance to help individuals navigate and stabilize their situations.

Grief and Loss:

Coping with the loss of a loved one or dealing with significant life changes can be incredibly difficult. We provide compassionate support to help individuals process their grief and find ways to heal and move forward.

Our Approach :


At HENU, we recognize the importance of mental health and personal development in enhancing the quality of life for older community members. Our approach to counseling and skills building focuses on providing comprehensive support and practical skills to help individuals cope with challenges, build resilience, and thrive.

Holistic Support:

Addressing the emotional, psychological, and practical needs of each individual to foster overall well-being.

Personalized Care:

Tailoring our counseling services to meet the unique needs and goals of each client, ensuring a supportive and effective experience.

Group Sessions and Workshops:

Engage with others facing similar challenges or goals in our group sessions and workshops. These collaborative settings offer additional support, shared experiences, and a sense of community.

Skill Building:

Offering targeted skills development programs that enhance self-sufficiency and open new opportunities for personal and professional growth.



At HENU, we are dedicated to making a meaningful difference in the lives of the elderly community we serve. If you or someone you know could benefit from our specialized counseling services or skills development programs tailored for seniors, we encourage you to reach out. Whether you're seeking emotional support, aiming to enhance your skills, or need guidance on navigating life's challenges, our dedicated team is here to help.
