Welcome to the Health Key Area

Welcome to the Health Key Area at Health Nest Uganda (HENU), where we prioritize the well-being and vitality of our older community members. Our commitment is to promote healthy lifestyles that enhance physical, mental, and social health. At HENU, we believe that every individual deserves to enjoy a vibrant and fulfilling life, no matter their age.

HENU: Encouraging Healthy Lifestyles for Older Community Members

HENU is dedicated to promoting healthy lifestyles among older community members to ensure their well-being and happiness.
We offer a range of programs designed to enhance physical health, mental wellness, and social engagement.
Our initiatives include tailored exercise programs, mindfulness sessions, social events, and nutritional guidance. By fostering a supportive and active community, we help older adults lead fulfilling and joyful lives.



Health health approach

Our Approach to Health

Our approach to health at HENU is holistic and integrative, aiming to bridge the gap between informal and formal health care services. We focus on providing accessible, quality care that addresses the diverse health needs of the elderly. By fostering partnerships and community involvement, we aim to create a sustainable and supportive health care network.

Key strategies

Facilitating Access to Health Services:

We work to ensure that elderly individuals have access to necessary health services, including regular check-ups, medication, and specialist consultations. This includes partnering with local health facilities and mobile clinics to reach those in remote areas.

Home Care Support

Our home care support program provides trained volunteers and paid care assistants to assist the elderly with daily tasks, medication management, and health monitoring. This personalized care helps maintain their independence and well-being.

Health Education and Awareness

We conduct health education campaigns to inform the elderly and their caregivers about common health issues, preventive measures, and healthy lifestyle choices. Workshops and seminars cover topics such as nutrition, chronic disease management, and mental health.

Mental Health and Emotional Support

Recognizing the importance of mental health, we offer counseling services, support groups, and recreational activities to address issues such as depression, anxiety, and social isolation. These services help improve the overall quality of life for the elderly.

Health Screenings and Preventive Care

Regular health screenings and preventive care are essential for early detection and treatment of health issues. We organize screening camps and preventive care sessions to ensure that the elderly receive timely medical attention.

Our comprehensive approach to health care for the elderly ensures that they receive the support and services they need to live healthy and fulfilling lives. By integrating personal and environmental hygiene practices, improving sanitation facilities, managing waste effectively, and raising awareness, we create a healthier community for the elderly.


Covid interventions

Covid 19 Intervetion

During the COVID-19 pandemic, HENU took significant steps to support older community members in Entebbe Sub-region. We provided essential supplies and vital health information to mitigate the impact of the pandemic on the elderly.


Our interventions included distributing :
  • Soap Distribution: 1,620 older persons supported with soap in 30 villages of Entebbe Sub-region
  • Food Aid: 506 older persons received posho and 172 received sugar
  • Health Education:COVID-19 health messages were disseminated during soap distribution.
  • Combating Isolation:1,523 older persons were reached by telephone to reduce isolation.
  • Digital Inclusion:Formed a WhatsApp group for older persons with smartphones to facilitate access to information.
