Intergenerational Self-Help Groups (ISHGs)

At HENU, our Intergenerational Self-Help Groups (ISHGs) are dedicated to combatting loneliness—a significant issue that can be as detrimental to health as smoking—by fostering meaningful connections across generations. We believe that bringing together individuals from different age groups can create a supportive network that enhances well-being and combats isolation.

Combatting Loneliness: Loneliness has been shown to have serious health implications, and our ISHGs are designed to address this by building strong, supportive relationships between seniors and younger participants. By creating opportunities for regular interaction and mutual support, we aim to reduce feelings of isolation and improve overall quality of life.

Collaborating on the Pictures of Ageing Project: We are excited to be partnering with the International Development Institute (IDI) on the Pictures of Ageing Project in Busukuma. This initiative uses photography to capture and share the diverse experiences of aging, fostering greater understanding and appreciation of seniors' lives. Through this collaboration, we aim to highlight the value of intergenerational connections and the impact they have on reducing loneliness and promoting emotional health.

ISHGs Key Components

HENU's ISHGs Key Components:


Our Intergenerational Self-Help Groups strive to create a vibrant and supportive community where individuals of all ages can connect, learn, and grow together.


Connection Building:

Facilitate interactions between different generations to provide emotional support and companionship, addressing loneliness and enhancing social networks.

Home Care Volunteers:

Engage volunteers to assist with in-home care needs, offering practical support and companionship to seniors while building intergenerational bonds.

Paid Care Assistants:

Provide professional care assistants to ensure that seniors receive the necessary support and services to maintain their health and well-being.

Raising Awareness and Knowledge:

Educate the community about aging issues and the benefits of intergenerational connections to foster greater understanding and support.

Risk and Disaster Management:

Implement strategies and resources to help seniors prepare for and manage risks and disasters, ensuring their safety and resilience.

Spiritual Support:

Offer spiritual and emotional support through counseling and community engagement, addressing the holistic needs of seniors.

Rights and Entitlements:

Provide information and advocacy on seniors' rights and entitlements, helping them access necessary services and benefits.

Self-Help Community Support:

Promote self-help initiatives within the community to empower seniors and other participants to take an active role in their support and development.

Income Generation Activities:

Facilitate income-generating opportunities for seniors and other participants to enhance financial stability and independence.

Resource Mobilization:

Work to mobilize resources and support from various stakeholders to sustain and expand our ISHG initiatives, ensuring ongoing impact and effectiveness.

Community Engagement:

Promote joint community projects and activities that strengthen ties within the community and offer opportunities for collaboration and support.

Health and Wellness:

Address the physical and emotional aspects of health through group activities, wellness programs, and shared experiences.

Through these focused efforts, HENU's ISHGs are making a significant impact in the lives of our older community members. Together, we are building a supportive, inclusive, and vibrant community where seniors can thrive. Join us in our mission to create a brighter, healthier, and more connected
